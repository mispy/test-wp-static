In addition we would like to thank the 139 supporters who donated to the crowdfunding campaign on Tilt: In November 2015 James Beshara started a one week crowdfunding campaign to “help save OurWorldInData.org” on the crowdfunding platform Tilt.com. At the time we did not have enough financial means to continue our work and we were extremely grateful for his initiative.
We were delighted that so many more people than expected gave their financial support. After one week 139 people joined the campaign and together supported Our World In Data with $26,086. All 139 individual donors are listed at the crowdfunding campaign: tilt.com/tilts/help-save-ourworldindataorg

We are very grateful for this vital support of our work and would like to thank all readers who make our work possible!

We received support from 2015 to 2016 from the London-based Nuffield Foundation for which we were very grateful.

Your support makes this free publication on global development possible.
