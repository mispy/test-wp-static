Mental health - new entry now online

April 23, 2018 by Hannah Ritchie

In recent months we have been working on a new entry on Mental Health. This entry is now available to explore.

In this new entry we present data visualisations and explainers on the prevalence of mental health disorders across the world, including depression, anxiety, bipolar, eating disorders and schizophrenia. In addition, we look at the relationships between mental health disorders and its risk factors, life satisfaction, substance use disorders, and suicide.

Our work is always in continuous development as new data and research emerges; we will continue to update this entry in the future.

Visit our new entry on Mental Health (here).
