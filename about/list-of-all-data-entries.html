This is the list of the 275 topics for which we have gathered data and that we cover or plan to cover on OurWorldInData.

 

Our World In Data is a work in progress. Only few data entries are completed – you find them marked by a star on the front page of OurWorldInData.org.

 

For all topics marked by a "d" a preliminary drafts for the future data-entry is already online: In these cases we do already present some research, but the data-entries are often far from completion. These are merely preliminary drafts for the future data entries on Our World In Data and already do include some data visualisations and more rarely we present some aspects of the research literature on this topic.
